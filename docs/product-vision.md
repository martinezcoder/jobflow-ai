# Product Vision

## Vision

JobFlow AI is an AI-powered career companion that helps people build a professional life aligned with the life they want to live.

Instead of simply tracking job applications, JobFlow AI understands each person's experience, aspirations, values, lifestyle preferences, and long-term goals to provide personalized guidance throughout their career journey.

## The Problem

Most career tools focus on managing job applications.

They assume users already know:
- what kind of job they want,
- where to look,
- how to prepare,
- and whether an opportunity truly fits them.

In reality, many people struggle much earlier.

They don't know which career paths fit their profile, which skills to develop next, or how to align their professional decisions with the life they want to build.

## Our Solution

JobFlow AI combines artificial intelligence, automation, and career data to help users:

- Understand themselves better.
- Discover career opportunities.
- Evaluate how well opportunities fit their goals.
- Prepare for applications and interviews.
- Learn the skills that matter most.
- Reflect on their career and continuously grow.

The application acts as a long-term career companion rather than a job application tracker.

## Principles

- User goals come before job opportunities.
- Career decisions should align with lifestyle goals.
- AI should guide, not replace, human decisions.
- Data should be collected through natural conversations whenever possible.
- Automation should reduce repetitive work, not user control.

## What We Are Not

JobFlow AI is not:
- another ATS (Applicant Tracking System),
- another Kanban board for job applications,
- another CV builder.

It is a decision-support system for career development.
