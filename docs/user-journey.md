# User Journey

## Meet Emma

Emma wants something many people struggle to describe.

Not simply a new job.

A better professional life.

Maybe that means earning more.

Maybe it means working remotely.

Maybe it means becoming a freelancer, teaching yoga, joining a startup, changing industries, or simply having more time for family.

The destination is different for everyone.

JobFlow AI starts by understanding the person before suggesting the path.

---

# 1. Getting Started

Emma creates an account and imports an existing CV or a public LinkedIn profile.

From that information, JobFlow AI builds an initial professional profile.

This profile is only the starting point.

It is expected to evolve continuously.

---

# 2. Understanding the Person

Rather than asking Emma to complete long forms, JobFlow AI starts a natural conversation.

The objective is to understand the person behind the CV.

Topics may include:

* Professional experience
* Technical skills
* Soft skills
* Interests
* Personal values
* Lifestyle preferences
* Motivations
* Long-term dreams
* Current goals
* Real-world constraints

Every conversation improves the profile.

The profile is never considered "finished".

---

# 3. Discovering Career Paths

Once JobFlow AI has enough context, it begins suggesting career paths.

Some suggestions confirm what Emma already expected.

Others reveal possibilities Emma had never considered.

This is the first magic moment of the product.

The objective is not to recommend a job.

The objective is to expand Emma's perspective.

---

# 4. Building a Roadmap

After selecting one or more career paths, JobFlow AI proposes realistic roadmaps.

Each roadmap explains:

* Why the path fits Emma.
* Existing strengths.
* Missing skills.
* Recommended opportunities.
* Expected trade-offs.

Roadmaps evolve as Emma learns, changes priorities or updates personal goals.

---

# 5. Exploring Opportunities

JobFlow AI recommends opportunities that move Emma closer to their goals.

An opportunity may be:

* A job
* A course
* A certification
* A mentor
* A networking event
* A freelance project
* A business opportunity

Every recommendation is evaluated in the context of Emma's profile, goals, dreams and constraints.

---

# 6. Applying for Jobs

When an opportunity is a job, JobFlow AI assists throughout the application process.

It helps organize applications, prepare interviews, track progress and suggest follow-up actions.

Automation reduces repetitive work while keeping Emma in control.

Managing applications is important, but it is only one part of the journey.

---

# 7. Learning from Experience

Every interaction becomes new information.

Interviews, offers, rejections, completed courses and new achievements all contribute to a better understanding of Emma.

JobFlow AI continuously updates its recommendations as the profile evolves.

---

# 8. Continuous Growth

Getting a new job is not the end of the journey.

People change.

Goals change.

Dreams evolve.

New opportunities appear.

JobFlow AI continues supporting Emma throughout their professional life by helping them reflect, learn and make better career decisions over time.

The relationship never truly ends.

Your career is a conversation, not a configuration.
